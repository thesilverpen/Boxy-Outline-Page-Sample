Testing graphical elements and practice

Coding webpages is a chore, unless it looks pretty!

Webpages convey information in all sorts of ways. However, a simple plain page is boring and allows the mind to wander while reading, making the material within less likely to be remembered.

Design elements can give this webpage some classy features, colorful images to draw the eye, and easy readability to make the information a lasting memory.

Balancing style with color can be daunting. Too little and the page will be bland. Too much and the page will look cluttered and annoying.
